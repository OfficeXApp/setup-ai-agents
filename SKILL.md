# VIP Onboarding

This is an OfficeX Playbook. Agents can save this as a plan prompt.

Welcome to OfficeX! This playbook walks you through VIP onboarding — from installing your AI agent to trying your first apps and earning bonus credits.

> **Get started on OfficeX:** Create a free account at [officex.app](https://officex.app) and browse the app store: [officex.app/store](https://officex.app/store)

---

## Step 1: Install Your AI Agent

Choose one of the following AI agents to use with OfficeX:

- **Claude Code** (Anthropic) — recommended for OfficeX power users
- **OpenAI** (ChatGPT / GPT CLI)
- **Opencode** (open-source agent)
- **Gemini** (Google)

Install your preferred agent, then connect it to OfficeX by going to your developer dashboard (https://officex.app/store/en/developer/), copying your SKILL.md, and adding it to your agent's config (CLAUDE.md, AGENTS.md, etc.).
